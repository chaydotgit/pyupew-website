The Process

Inquiry
Agreement
Creation
Payment
Delivery
  1. Inquiry: During the inquiry phase, you will contact me and provide the information needed in order to create your commission. Below there is a template that you can fill out to streamline the process and make it easier!
  2. Agreement: I will review your commission request. I will discuss the request with you and what I am able and not able to do if needed. Once we reach an agreement on the content of the commission and the price, I will be able to start your commission! Throughout this I have the right to decline your request at any time.
  3. Creation: Throughout the creation of your commission, I will update you for approval and ask questions regarding the content if necessary. This phase also includes revisions if there is something you are not happy with.
  4. Payment: Payment is needed before the commission is finished in order to receive the final product. I take payment through Stripe (preferred). Payment is also taken through Paypal, however the commissioner is responsible for any extra fees.
  5. Delivery: Once payment is received and you are happy with your commission, you will receive the artwork in png! You can also receive it in another file format if requested.
